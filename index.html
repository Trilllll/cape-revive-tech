Why upgrade instead of replace?

Most “slow computer” problems come from two bottlenecks: storage (old hard drive) and memory (not enough RAM).

Speed
Biggest improvement, fastest
An SSD upgrade is the single most noticeable upgrade for older systems. Boot times, loading, and everyday responsiveness improve immediately.
Comfort
Keep your files + setup
With a drive transfer, you keep your accounts, documents, and layout. Same computer, same setup — just faster.
Value
Spend smarter
Upgrades are usually a fraction of the cost of a replacement laptop. I’ll tell you if an upgrade is worth it before you spend money.
Common “slow computer” symptoms
Long boot time Freezing with tabs/apps Programs open slowly Disk usage stuck at 100%
